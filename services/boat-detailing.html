FAQ

Do you need water or power?

We are self-contained for most boat jobs. If marina rules require hookups, we'll confirm those details at booking.

Can you work on boats in the water?

We primarily work on boats on trailers or in dry storage. For boats in slips, we can perform limited topside services where marina rules allow.

How often should I detail my boat?

We recommend a full detail at the beginning and end of each season, with wash & wax services every 1-2 months during heavy use.

Do you work on jet skis and PWCs?

Yes! We detail all personal watercraft. Jet ski detailing starts at $150 for basic wash & wax.

What's the difference between automotive and marine products?

Marine products are specifically formulated to handle water exposure, salt, and UV rays. They're essential for proper boat care.
