Paint Touch-Up FAQ

How long does paint touch-up take?

Single chips: 30-45 minutes. Multiple chips: 1-2 hours. Scratch repair: 1-3 hours depending on size. Most services completed same day.

Will the repair be visible?

Our professional technique creates nearly invisible repairs. While not 100% perfect like a full respray, most repairs are unnoticeable from 3 feet away.

Do you guarantee color match?

Yes! We use your VIN to get the exact factory paint code and professional automotive paint for perfect color matching.

Can you fix any paint damage?

We handle chips, scratches, and minor damage. Large dents, extensive rust, or damage requiring body work needs a body shop.

How soon should I fix rock chips?

ASAP! Exposed metal can start rusting within days, especially during monsoon season. Early repair prevents spreading.

Is touch-up better than repainting?

For minor damage, absolutely! Touch-up preserves your original factory paint and costs significantly less than panel repainting.

Do you warranty your work?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on all touch-up repairs against peeling, fading, or color mismatch.
