FAQ

Will correction remove all scratches?

Deep scratches that catch a fingernail may be beyond safe polishing limits. We aim for the best result while preserving clear coat.

How long does it take?

Enhancement: 3–5 hrs; Correction: 5–8 hrs; Show Car: 1–2 days depending on size and paint hardness.

Do you offer wet sanding?

Yes, case‑by‑case after inspection. Quoted separately for safety.
