PPF vs Ceramic Coating

Paint Protection Film

  • Stops rock chips & sand blast
  • Self‑heals light swirls with heat
  • Thick TPU layer (sacrificial)
  • Optional gloss or matte finish

Ceramic Coating

  • Makes washing easier
  • Enhances gloss & slickness
  • Does not stop rock chips
  • Perfect topper for PPF

After‑Care

  • No high‑pressure washing for 48–72 hours
  • Use pH‑neutral soap and plush mitts
  • Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive polishes
  • Annual inspection & decon recommended

Warranty

We install premium, manufacturer‑warrantied films. Typical coverage includes yellowing, cracking, bubbling, and adhesive failure under normal use.

FAQ

Will you wrap edges?

Where safe and feasible, we wrap or relieve edges for a clean look. Some edges cannot be wrapped due to panel design.

Do you remove old film?

Yes—old film removal is available and quoted separately based on condition and adhesive residue.

Gloss or matte?

Both are available. Matte PPF gives a stealth look; gloss is nearly invisible and enhances depth.
