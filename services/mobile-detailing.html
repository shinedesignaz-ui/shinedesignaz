Mobile Detailing FAQ

Common questions about our mobile service

Do you bring your own water and power?

Yes! Our mobile units are fully self-contained with water tanks, generators, and all equipment. You don't need to provide anything - we just need access to your vehicle.

Can you detail in my apartment complex?

Absolutely! We work in apartment complexes, office buildings, and residential driveways. We'll work with your HOA or property management requirements.

What if it rains?

We monitor weather closely. If rain is expected, we'll contact you to reschedule. Light rain usually isn't an issue as we can work under carports or use our pop-up canopy.

How do I prepare my vehicle?

Simply remove personal items and valuables. Leave about 3 feet of space around the vehicle if possible. We handle everything else including pre-inspection photos.

Is mobile detailing more expensive?

Our mobile prices are competitive with shop services. The convenience of at-home service often saves you money in time and gas, making it a great value.

Do you offer recurring service?

Yes! We offer monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly maintenance plans with discounted rates. Perfect for keeping your vehicle pristine year-round.
