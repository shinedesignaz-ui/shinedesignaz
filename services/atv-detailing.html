ATV Detailing FAQ

How long does ATV detailing take?

Trail Wash: 45-60 minutes. Deep Detail: 1.5-2 hours. Show Ready: 2.5-3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

Do you need water or power hookups?

No! We bring our own water tank and generator. We're completely self-contained and can detail anywhere.

Can you remove all the mud from my last ride?

Yes! We specialize in removing caked-on mud, even from hard-to-reach areas like radiators and engine bays.

Do you detail dirt bikes too?

Absolutely! We detail all off-road vehicles including dirt bikes, motorcycles, and even golf carts.

Is ceramic coating worth it for ATVs?

Yes! Ceramic coating makes future cleaning much easier and protects against UV damage, especially important in Arizona.

Can you come to my storage unit?

Yes, we service homes, storage facilities, shops, and anywhere else you keep your toys in the East Valley.
