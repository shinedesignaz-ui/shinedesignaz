Motorcycle Detailing FAQ

How long does motorcycle detailing take?

Basic Detail: 1.5-2 hours. Full Detail: 3-4 hours. Ceramic Coating: 6-8 hours (may require leaving overnight).

Do you detail at motorcycle dealerships?

Yes! We work with several dealerships and can detail at your location, dealership, or our shop.

Can you remove exhaust bluing?

Yes, we can significantly reduce or eliminate bluing on chrome pipes using specialized products and techniques.

Is ceramic coating worth it for motorcycles?

Absolutely. It makes cleaning much easier, protects against UV damage, and maintains that showroom shine longer.

Do you detail custom bikes and choppers?

Yes! We love custom builds and understand the unique care they require. We'll treat your custom with the respect it deserves.

Can you detail at rallies or events?

Yes, we offer on-site detailing for rallies, bike nights, and events. Contact us for group rates.
