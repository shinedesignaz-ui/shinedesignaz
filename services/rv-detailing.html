FAQ

Do you need hookups?

No! We bring our own water and power generator. We just need access to your RV.

Can you detail at storage facilities?

Yes! We service RVs at homes, storage facilities, RV parks, and dealerships throughout the East Valley.

How often should I detail my RV?

We recommend a thorough wash every 3-4 months, waxing twice yearly, and ceramic coating every 2-5 years for maximum protection.

Do you detail RV roofs?

Yes! We clean and treat rubber, fiberglass, and aluminum roofs. We also offer professional roof resealing services.

What's the difference between spray wax and machine wax?

Spray wax provides 1-2 months of protection and is great for maintenance. Machine wax lasts 6+ months with deeper protection and superior shine.
