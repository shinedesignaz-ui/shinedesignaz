Why Ceramic in Arizona?

Our climate is brutal: sun, dust, sprinklers, and freeway debris. Ceramic coatings deliver a hardened sacrificial layer that resists UV, makes washes faster, and keeps paint glossier for years.

  • Hydrophobic top-coat sheds dust and water spots
  • UV inhibitors fight oxidation and clear-coat failure
  • Enhances color depth and mirror-like reflections
  • Reduces washing frequency and effort
  • Protection verified and added to vehicle's CARFAX report

Our Process

  1. Decon wash, iron removal, and clay bar to strip contaminants
  2. Tape-off trim and perform appropriate paint prep (light polish, enhancement, or correction)
  3. Panel wipe to remove polishing oils for proper bond
  4. Apply ceramic coating and level for a uniform, slick finish
  5. IR cure (where applicable) and final inspection
  6. Document service and add to CARFAX vehicle history

FAQ

How long before I can wash the car?

Gentle hand-wash after 7 days; avoid harsh chemicals for 2 weeks while the coating fully cures.

Will a coating stop rock chips?

No—use PPF for impact protection. Many clients do PPF up front and ceramic over the rest.

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes—monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly plans keep performance at its peak. See Pricing.

What's the difference between the packages?

Essential includes light polish, Premium includes enhancement polish, and Elite includes full paint correction. Higher tiers also use more advanced coating systems with longer durability.

Is the CARFAX addition worth it?

Absolutely. It documents professional ceramic coating on your vehicle's history report, which can increase resale value and buyer confidence.
