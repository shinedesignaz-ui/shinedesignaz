Paint Protection Film Near Me - FAQ

Where can I get paint protection film near me?

Shine Design offers professional PPF installation throughout East Valley AZ. We have a facility in Gilbert and provide mobile service to Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, and surrounding areas. We're XPEL and 3M certified installers. Call (480) 528-8227.

How much does paint protection film cost near me?

PPF installation starts at $599 for partial front coverage, $1,999 for front end packages, $3,999 for track packages, and $5,999 for full vehicle coverage. Price varies by vehicle size and coverage area. All installations include 10-year warranty.

Is paint protection film worth it?

Yes! PPF provides invisible protection against rock chips, scratches, bird droppings, and UV damage. The self-healing technology repairs minor scratches automatically. With a 10-year warranty, it protects your paint and maintains resale value. It's especially valuable for new cars, daily drivers on highways, and luxury vehicles.

How long does PPF installation take?

Installation time varies by coverage: Partial front (4-6 hours), Full front (8-10 hours), Track package (1-2 days), Full vehicle (2-3 days). We can arrange loaner vehicles or Uber credits for longer installations.

Can PPF be removed?

Yes, PPF can be safely removed without damaging the original paint. Professional removal is recommended and typically takes 2-4 hours depending on coverage. The film is designed to protect the paint underneath and leaves no residue when properly removed.

What's the difference between PPF and ceramic coating?

PPF is a physical barrier (8mil thick film) that protects against rock chips and scratches. Ceramic coating is a chemical barrier (2-3 microns) that provides hydrophobic properties and UV protection. Many customers choose both for ultimate protection - PPF on high-impact areas and ceramic coating on all painted surfaces.
