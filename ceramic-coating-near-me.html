How long does ceramic coating last?

Professional ceramic coating lasts 3-7+ years depending on the package selected. We offer 3-year, 5-year, and lifetime protection options. All come with warranty and annual maintenance programs.

Is ceramic coating worth it?

Yes! Ceramic coating protects your paint from UV damage, chemicals, bird droppings, and contaminants. It makes washing easier, maintains your car's appearance, and adds resale value. The CARFAX documentation proves the protection to future buyers.

Can you apply ceramic coating at my home?

Yes, we provide mobile ceramic coating service. We bring a controlled environment setup to ensure proper application conditions. The coating is applied and cured at your location with professional results.

Do I need paint correction before ceramic coating?

It depends on your paint condition. New cars may only need light polish, while older vehicles might need paint correction. All our packages include the necessary paint preparation for optimal coating adhesion and appearance.
