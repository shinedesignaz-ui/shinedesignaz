Ceramic Coating FAQ

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our Essential package lasts 3 years, Premium lasts 5+ years, and Elite provides 7+ years to lifetime protection with proper maintenance. All packages include warranty protection.

What's included in the paint correction?

Paint correction (Elite package) removes swirls, scratches, oxidation, and other imperfections using professional polishing techniques. This creates a flawless surface before coating application.

Can I wash my car normally after ceramic coating?

Yes! Ceramic coating makes washing easier. Use pH-neutral car shampoo and avoid automatic car washes with brushes. The hydrophobic properties mean less washing is needed.

Is ceramic coating worth it in Arizona?

Absolutely! Arizona's intense UV rays and heat accelerate paint damage. Ceramic coating provides superior UV protection, prevents oxidation, and makes dust/dirt removal much easier.

What's the difference between the packages?

Essential provides basic protection with light polish. Premium adds enhancement polish and trim coating. Elite includes full paint correction plus glass, trim, and wheel coating for maximum protection and value.
