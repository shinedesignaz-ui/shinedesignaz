Pricing FAQ

What's the difference between the packages?

Silver is for well-maintained vehicles needing basic cleaning. Gold includes deep cleaning and protection. Platinum is our complete restoration with maximum protection plus FREE engine bay and glass treatment.

How much can I save with bundles?

Bundle packages save 28-40% compared to individual services. Platinum package includes $118 in free add-ons!

How long does each service take?

Level 1: 1-2 hours, Level 2: 2-4 hours, Level 3: 4-6 hours. Bundle packages take 3-7 hours depending on size and condition.

Can I change my package after booking?

Yes! You can upgrade or modify your service up to 24 hours before your appointment.

Is there a mobile service fee?

No! Mobile service is free within our service area (Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley).
