Window Tinting FAQ

What's the legal tint limit in Arizona?

Arizona allows any darkness on rear windows. Front side windows must allow 33% light transmission. Windshield can have non-reflective tint above the AS-1 line (top 5 inches).

How long does installation take?

Most vehicles take 2-3 hours. We recommend not rolling down windows for 3-5 days to allow proper curing.

Will ceramic tint interfere with my electronics?

No! Unlike metallic tints, ceramic film won't interfere with GPS, cell phones, radio, or toll passes.

What's covered under the lifetime warranty?

The warranty covers peeling, cracking, fading, and delamination. It does not cover damage from seat belts, sharp objects, or improper cleaning.
