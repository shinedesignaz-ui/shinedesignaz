Exterior Detailing FAQ

How long does exterior detailing take?

Level 1: 1-1.5 hours. Level 2: 2-3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

What's the difference between Level 1 and Level 2?

Level 2 includes clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants, iron decontamination, and a 6-month paint sealant for long-lasting protection.

Do you use automated car wash equipment?

No, everything is done by hand using premium products and techniques to ensure your paint is never scratched.

How often should I get exterior detailing?

Level 1 monthly for maintenance, Level 2 every 3-6 months for protection, or before seasonal changes.

Can you remove water spots and swirls?

Level 2 can remove light water spots. For swirls and scratches, see our Paint Correction services.
