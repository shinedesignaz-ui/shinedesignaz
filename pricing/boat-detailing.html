Boat Detailing FAQ

How long does boat detailing take?

Basic wash: 2-3 hours. Full detail: 4-6 hours. Gelcoat restoration: 6-8 hours. Ceramic coating: 1-2 days depending on size and condition.

Do you service boats at marinas?

Yes! We service boats at marinas, private docks, storage facilities, and dry storage throughout the East Valley.

Can you detail while the boat is in the water?

Yes, we can detail boats in the water. For hull work and ceramic coating, the boat needs to be on a trailer or lift.

How often should I detail my boat?

We recommend washing monthly during boating season, full detail twice yearly, and ceramic coating every 2-3 years for maximum protection.

Do you detail jet skis and pontoons?

Yes! We detail all watercraft including jet skis, pontoons, sailboats, fishing boats, and yachts.

What's included in gelcoat restoration?

We remove oxidation, wet sand if needed, compound, polish, and seal the gelcoat to restore color and shine.
