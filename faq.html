General

Do you come to me?

Yes—we’re mobile across Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley and surrounding PHX. Shop drop‑off by request.

Are you insured?

Yes. We’re fully insured; we can provide a COI for HOAs, office parks and fleet clients.

What should I do before you arrive?

Please remove personal items and car seats if possible. We’ll take it from there.

Interior & Exterior

What’s included in an exterior detail?

Two‑bucket hand wash, wheels/tires, foam soak, dry, and protection. Level 2 adds iron decon, clay bar, bug/tar and 6‑month sealant.

What’s included in an interior detail?

Vacuum, wipe‑down, glass; steam and shampoo/extraction on deeper packages; leather clean & condition where applicable.

Can you remove pet hair and odors?

Yes—heavy pet hair and severe odors take extra time. Ozone is available after extraction for tough cases.

Paint Correction

Will correction remove all scratches?

Deep scratches that catch a fingernail may be beyond safe limits. We maximize results while protecting your clear coat.

How long does a correction take?

Enhancement: 3–5 hrs; Standard: 5–8 hrs; Show‑Car: 1–2 days depending on size/paint hardness.

Ceramic Coating

How long do coatings last?

We offer 1‑, 3‑ and 5‑year options. Longevity depends on prep and maintenance.

Do I need correction before coating?

At minimum, a gloss‑enhancing polish is recommended so we’re sealing in a great finish.

Does ceramic stop rock chips?

No—combine with PPF for impact protection.

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

What areas should I protect?

Most popular: Partial or Full Front (bumper, hood, fenders, mirrors). We can do rockers, luggage area and full body.

Is PPF self‑healing?

Yes—light swirls disappear with heat from the sun or warm water.

Window Tint

What film do you use?

Premium dyed and ceramic IR films with a lifetime warranty. Ceramic offers the best heat rejection and is signal‑safe.

Can you tint the windshield?

Yes—visor strips and compliant light shades are available. We’ll advise on what makes sense for your vehicle.

Booking & Payment

How do I book and get a quote?

Use our online booking to select a time, or tap “Free Quote” on any service. We’ll confirm by text.

What payment types do you take?

Cash, card and contactless. Fleets can be invoiced with statements.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem—reply to your confirmation text or contact us. Weather reschedules are always free.

After‑Care & Warranty

First‑wash guidance after a ceramic coating?

Wait a few days if possible; use pH‑neutral soap, plush mitts and dry with soft towels. Avoid harsh chemicals.

What’s covered under warranty?

Manufacturer warranty on films/coatings and workmanship on details. Excludes misuse (seat‑belt chips, ammonia cleaners, rolling windows before cure, etc.).
