Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025 • This policy explains how Shine Design, LLC ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our site, request a quote, or book a service.

On this page

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Information 3. SMS & Email 4. Cookies & Analytics 5. How We Share Information 6. Retention 7. Security 8. Your Choices 9. Children’s Privacy 10. Changes to This Policy 11. Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact & booking data you provide: name, phone, email, address, vehicle, service selections, preferred date/time, and notes.
  • Communications (texts/emails) related to quotes, confirmations, reminders, and follow‑ups.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser/device info, pages viewed, and interactions gathered via cookies or similar tools.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide quotes, schedule services, and deliver on‑site or drop‑off work.
  • Send confirmations, reminders, and updates about your appointment.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support.
  • Maintain records for business, accounting, legal, and security purposes.

3. SMS & Email

  • By providing your phone or email, you consent to receive messages about your quote/appointment (and occasional follow‑ups). Message/data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt‑out of SMS at any time, or use unsubscribe links in emails.

4. Cookies & Analytics

  • We may use cookies and similar technologies for essential site features and to understand site usage.
  • We may use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics/Tag Manager) to measure traffic and performance. See Google’s policies for how data is handled.

5. How We Share Information

  • Service providers (e.g., booking/CRM, payment processors, communications providers) who help us operate our business. They are permitted to use data only to provide services to us.
  • Legal, safety, and compliance purposes when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or customers.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

6. Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide services and for legitimate business or legal purposes. Retention periods vary based on the type of data and our obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

  • Update or request deletion of your contact/booking details by contacting us.
  • Manage cookies via your browser settings.
  • Opt‑out of SMS by replying STOP; opt‑out of marketing emails using unsubscribe links.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services and website are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date.

11. Contact Us

Questions or requests? Contact us or call/text (480) 528‑8227.
